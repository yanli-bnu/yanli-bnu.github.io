    Welcome to Dr. Yan Li's (李琰) website!
    Dr. Yan Li is a Professor of Faculty of Geographical Sciences at Beijing Normal University.
     I obtained Ph.D. from Peking University in 2015. I was a Postdoctoral researcher at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and University of Maryland, College Park from 2015 to 2018.
    My research interest lies in vegetation and climate interactions, climate change and agriculture. I have published more than 40 peer-reviewed articles including first author papers published in Science, Nature Communications, and Global Change Biology.

    If you have questions or seek for research collaboration, please contact me through email.
    Our lab is recruiting Postdocs, PhD, master students, and undergraduate interns, please feel free to contact me if you are interested in.
